Create navigational links within a website

There are several techniques to navigating within a website. The semantic tag for navigation is the <nav> tag. You can navigate through your website by using relative addressing. For our examples, we will us the unordered list tag (<ul>). Our example will consist of:

  • page to page within the same folder
  • page to page between folders
  • staying on the current page
  • going to a specific place on the page (also known as on-page navigation)
  • going to a specific place on another page

Navigational menus are placed in your <header> semantic area. They may also be placed in your <footer> area.
